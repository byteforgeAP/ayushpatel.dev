About Me

I’m Ayush Patel, a Computer Science student at Kent State University with a passion for both software development and creative technology. I specialize in C++ and Python, with a strong foundation in data structures, memory management, and algorithmic problem solving. My projects range from building custom dynamic classes and stack-based expression parsers to developing collaborative Pygame arcade-style games. Beyond coding, I have hands-on experience in video production and editing, creating accessible multimedia content with Adobe Premiere Pro. I enjoy blending technical precision with creativity whether it’s designing scalable software systems, crafting interactive game mechanics, or producing polished video content. I thrive in collaborative environments, leveraging Git-based workflows and structured project management to deliver high-quality results. I’m always exploring new ways to combine programming, UI/UX design, and multimedia storytelling to build engaging, scalable, and user-focused experiences.
